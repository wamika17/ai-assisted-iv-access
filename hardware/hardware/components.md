# Hardware Components

## ESP32

Acts as the central embedded controller responsible for acquiring sensor data, controlling peripherals, and communicating with the software application.

---

## Infrared Camera

Captures infrared images of superficial veins beneath the skin.

---

## MAX30102

Measures heart rate and blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂) using photoplethysmography (PPG).

---

## OLED Display

Displays device status, battery information, and patient measurements.

---

## Rechargeable Battery

Provides portable power for field and bedside operation.

---

## TP4056 Charging Module

Safely charges the lithium-ion battery while protecting against overcharging.
